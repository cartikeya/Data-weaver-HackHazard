Deep work is the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task, allowing you to learn and master hard things quickly, this concept is supported by the idea that to advance your understanding of a field you must tackle the relevant topics systematically, with intense concentration, the key to deep work is to create an environment that supports focus and eliminates distractions, this can be achieved by setting clear goals, scheduling large blocks of uninterrupted time, and avoiding multitasking, by doing so, you can train your mind to concentrate and stay focused, leading to increased productivity and better outcomes.
